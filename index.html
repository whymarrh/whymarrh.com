Sunken History of Grand Lake

A dam was built on Grand Lake in the 1920s and the resulting 11 metre rise in water level drastically altered the terrain both above and below the surface. Lake Maps NL set out to create a map of this lake in an attempt to discover the original shoreline and unveil some of the history preserved below the floodwaters.
